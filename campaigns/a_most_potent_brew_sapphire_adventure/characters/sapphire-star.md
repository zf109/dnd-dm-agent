## üìñ Character Concept

Sapphire Star earned her nickname after accidentally creating a spectacular light display that saved her village from bandits. Though small in stature, her magical talent and quick wit make her a formidable companion. She left her farming community to learn more about her gift, hoping to return one day as a proper wizard.

**Personality:**
- Cheerful and optimistic, sees adventure as exciting rather than dangerous
- Quick to help those in need, especially common folk
- Curious about magic but sometimes reckless in experimentation

**Ideal:** **Freedom** - Everyone deserves to live without oppression (Chaotic)

**Bond:** I seek to use my magic to protect ordinary people like those in my village

**Flaw:** I'm too trusting of those who appear to be in need

## Roleplaying Notes

**Voice/Mannerisms:**
- Speaks with excitement about magic and adventure
- Often mentions farming metaphors ("That's harder than plowing a rocky field!")
- Fidgets with components when thinking

**Combat Style:**
- Stays at range with Ray of Frost and crossbow
- Uses Sleep to disable groups of weaker enemies
- Burning Hands as last resort in close quarters
- Lucky trait makes her surprisingly hard to pin down

**Roleplay Hooks for A Most Potent Brew:**
- Excited to explore ancient wizard ruins ("Real wizard stuff!")
- Sympathetic to Glowkindle's business troubles (understands common folk's struggles)
- Fascinated by old magical equipment and wants to understand how it works
- Might try to befriend any magical creatures encountered
