# A Most Potent Brew - Locations

## Wizard's Tower Brewing Co.

**Description**: Successful craft brewery known for hoppy summer ales

**Visual Details**: A converted stone building with cheerful painted signs advertising seasonal brews. Copper kettles gleam behind glass windows, and the wooden sign creaks gently in the breeze. Warm golden light spills from diamond-paned windows.

**Sensory Elements**: Rich aromas of malt and hops, bubbling sounds of fermentation, comfortable warmth from the brewing fires

**Features**:
- Tasting room (where Glowkindle meets the party)
- Brewing equipment (copper kettles, fermentation vats)
- Expanded cellars (recently dug, where the trouble started)

**Story Function**: Starting location and safe base

---

## The Brewery Cellars

**Description**: Stone cellars with beer barrels, recently expanded

**Visual Details**: Rough-hewn stone walls lined with oak barrels of various sizes. A newer section shows fresh mortar and tool marks where workers expanded the space. A ragged hole in the far wall reveals impenetrable darkness beyond.

**Sensory Elements**: Cool, damp air tinged with yeast and alcohol, echoing drips, scratching sounds from the shadows

**Features**:
- Rows of aging beer barrels
- Hole in wall leading to ancient ruins
- Scattered brewing supplies and tools
- Evidence of rat activity (droppings, claw marks)

**Story Function**: Entry point to adventure, evidence of rat problem

---

## Ancient Wizard Tower Ruins (Underground)

**Description**: 4-5 room dungeon with laboratory, living quarters, and storage areas

**Visual Details**: Carved stone corridors with faded magical runes, chambers filled with crystalline growths and arcane apparatus. Dust motes dance in shafts of mysterious phosphorescent light. Ancient furniture sits covered in decades of undisturbed dust.

**Sensory Elements**: Musty air with metallic tang of old magic, faint humming of dormant enchantments, occasional distant drips echoing through hidden chambers

**Features**:
- **Entry Chamber**: Collapsed ceiling where brewery workers broke through
- **Main Laboratory**: Alchemical equipment, potion ingredients, spell notes
- **Living Quarters**: Ancient furniture, personal effects of long-dead wizard
- **Storage Vaults**: Treasure, magical components, preserved specimens
- **Hidden Study**: Secret room with spellbooks and research notes

**Story Function**: Main adventure location, source of mystery, treasure location

**Dungeon Hazards**:
- Unstable floors (some areas weakened by time)
- Lingering magical effects (random minor magical phenomena)
- Trapped areas (simple traps protecting valuable items)

---

## Surrounding Area

### Town/Village (Nearby)
**Description**: Small settlement where the brewery is located
**Relevance**: Source of customers, potential for follow-up adventures

### Old Tower Site (Surface Ruins)
**Description**: Crumbling foundation stones mark where the wizard's tower once stood
**Details**: Overgrown with vegetation, locals avoid it due to superstitions
**Connection**: The underground ruins are all that remains after the tower collapsed centuries ago
